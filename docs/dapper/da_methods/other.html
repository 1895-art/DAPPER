def laplace_lklhd(xx)

Compute a Laplacian likelihood.

Compute likelihood of xx wrt. the sampling distribution RVs.LaplaceParallelRV(C=I), i.e., for x in xx: p(x) = exp(-sqrt(2)*|x|_1) / sqrt(2).
